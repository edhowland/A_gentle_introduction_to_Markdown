Text Spans

Spans are inline flow

Special characters are used to surround words to be highlighted within an outer context like a paragraph.

Span types

Bold

The following phrase is in **Bold Text**. Here is more __bold text__.

Becomes:

The following phrase is in Bold Text. Here is more bold text.

Italics

To place some words in _italics_, just use one star or underline like *this*.

Becomes:

To place some words in italics, just use one star or underline like this.

Combinations

Use 3 stars or underlines to make text both ***bold and italicized***.

Becomes:

Use 3 stars or underlines to make text both bold and italicized.
