Comments

All writers and programmers should know how insert comments in their source documents and code. Comments are for the author and future maintainers of the source file and will not appear in the compiled output.

Here is the most general form of comment understood by most Markdown processors:

[//]: # (This text should not appear in the output)

Note: Be sure to include a blank line both before and after the comment line.
